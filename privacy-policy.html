Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Falling Floor ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile game application Falling Floor (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We may collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Game progress and scores
  • In-game preferences and settings
  • Character selections and customizations

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our App, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • Game performance data
  • Usage statistics

2.3 Third-Party Services

Our App uses third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Mobile Ads: We use Google Mobile Ads SDK to display advertisements. Google may collect device identifiers and usage data. Please review Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Game Center: We use Apple's Game Center for leaderboards and achievements. Apple may collect your Game Center account information. Please review Apple's Privacy Policy at https://www.apple.com/privacy/

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our App
  • Personalize your gaming experience
  • Display leaderboards and achievements through Game Center
  • Show relevant advertisements
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns

4. Data Storage

Your game data (scores, preferences, character selections) is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. We do not transmit this data to our servers unless you choose to sync through Game Center.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With service providers who assist in operating our App (e.g., Google for ads, Apple for Game Center)

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored locally on your device
  • Delete your game data by uninstalling the App
  • Opt-out of personalized ads through your device settings
  • Disable Game Center features through your device settings

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy
